Revolutionizing surveillance with AI-powered intelligence and proactive security solutions

AI-Powered Intelligence

VigilantView is an advanced real-time surveillance solution powered by artificial intelligence. Built with security, accuracy, and scalability in mind, VigilantView is designed to detect violence and alert authorities instantly.

🟢

Proactive Security

The system uses a pretrained YOLO model to analyze live video feeds. Once violence is detected, it captures a clip and pushes real-time alerts while data is securely stored for later review.
